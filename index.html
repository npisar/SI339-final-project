"Tell me about that drum thing you do"

It's really pretty simple.

I've loved playing the snare drum since 5th grade, and specifically the marching snare drum since 7th grade. I've taken that passion to an extreme most people don't know exists.

During the summer and winter, I rehearse and perform with Winter Guard International (WGI) and Drum Corps International (DCI) ensembles, respectively. In short, those are governing bodies that organize professional marching bands or indoor drumline ensembles scattered around the country.
